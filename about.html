INCORPORATION

Tamil Nadu Urban Finance and Infrastructure Development Corporation Ltd (TUFIDCO) was incorporated under the Companies Act, 1956 on 21-03-1990. The main object of the corporation is providing financial assistance and guidance to Local Bodies, Corporations, Boards, Authorities and parastatal agencies for their development schemes. It is also a Nodal Agency to implement Government programme/schemes in the State. The Corporate Identification Number (CIN) of
TUFIDCO is "U65191TN1990SGC018909". TUFIDCO is the Nodal Agency for the following schemes Singara Chennai 2.0 Kalaignar Nagarpura Mempattu Thittam (KNMT)
